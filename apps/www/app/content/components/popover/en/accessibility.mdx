There is built-in accessibility in the [Popover API (mozilla.org)](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Popover_API) for open and closed states and for keyboard navigation.

### Keyboard navigation
* <kbd>Enter</kbd> or <kbd>Space</kbd> opens or closes
* <kbd>Esc</kbd> closes
