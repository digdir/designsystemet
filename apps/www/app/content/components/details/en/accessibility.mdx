For the `Details` component, we use [u-details (github.io)](https://u-elements.github.io/u-elements/elements/u-details), which builds on the semantic HTML elements `<details>` and `<summary>`.

## Keyboard navigation
Press <kbd>Space</kbd> or <kbd>Enter</kbd> to open or close the Details.
