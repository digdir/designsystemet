Tags are labels that can be used to categorize elements or communicate progress, status, or process. Tags are static labels; they cannot be clicked.

## Usage

```tsx
import { Tag } from '@digdir/designsystemet-react';
```

## Guidelines

Use `Tag` when you want to help users quickly identify content based on category, status, or property.

**Suitable for:**

- Categorizing content, such as "Beta", "New" or "Popular".
- Showing status, such as "In Progress", "Completed" or "Canceled".

**Not suitable for**

- linking to other pages (use [`Link`](/docs/komponenter-link--docs) instead)
- actions (use [`Button`](/docs/komponenter-button--docs) instead)
- filtering (use [`Chip`](/docs/komponenter-chip--docs) instead)
