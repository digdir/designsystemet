## Guidelines

`Heading` is used to create structure and hierarchy in content. Use headings to divide content into logical sections.

**Suitable for:**

- Structuring content
- Creating visual hierarchy
- Helping users navigate content

## Accessibility

It's important to use the correct heading level (h1-h6) to ensure good semantic structure.
